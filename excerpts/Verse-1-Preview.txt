Shehar diyan rahan utte driving so blind,
Teri hi sochan bas on my mind.
Kach de glass vich kholda main khaab,
Sohniye de de tu honest jawaab.
Rang-birangi roshni ch fading your face,
Gawach gaya haan main in this empty space.
Teri nishaniyan nu burning tonight,
Dil vich labhda haan catching the light.
